Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a screenshot?

Tap the "+ Add Frame" button to add a new device frame, then tap on the frame to select an image from your photo library.

How do I move or resize a frame?

Drag to move, pinch to resize, and use two fingers to rotate any frame or caption on the canvas. Double-tap to reset position.

How do I change the export size?

Tap the resize button next to the Export button to choose from different App Store screenshot dimensions for iPhone or iPad.

How do I export my screenshot?

Tap the "Export to Photos" button at the bottom. The image will be saved to your photo library at the optimal resolution for the App Store.

What's included in Store Spark Pro?

Pro subscribers get unlimited device frames, unlimited captions, and photo backgrounds with blur effects.

How do I restore my purchase?

Open the upgrade screen and tap "Restore Purchases" at the bottom. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Go to Settings → Your Name → Subscriptions on your device to manage or cancel.
