In short: Store Spark is a completely private app. We don't collect, store, or share any of your personal information. All image processing happens locally on your device.

Introduction

Store Spark ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our screenshot creation application.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

Store Spark is designed with privacy in mind:

  • All image processing happens locally on your device
  • We do not have access to your photos or screenshots
  • We do not track your usage or behavior
  • We do not use analytics or third-party tracking services
  • We do not collect device identifiers

Photos and Images

Store Spark requires access to your photo library to:

  • Import screenshots you want to frame
  • Import background images
  • Save your created images

All images remain on your device and are never uploaded to any server.

Purchases

Subscription purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. Apple's privacy policy governs the handling of payment data.

Data Storage

Store Spark does not store any data on external servers. All app data is stored locally on your device.

Third-Party Services

Store Spark does not use any third-party services, including:

  • No analytics services
  • No advertising networks
  • No crash reporting services
  • No cloud storage services

Children's Privacy

Store Spark does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app is suitable for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
