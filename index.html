Getting Started

Thy Word works completely offline. Once installed, you have full access to:

  • Complete Bible text in 5 translations
  • Complete Strong's Concordance (Hebrew & Greek)
  • All definitions and word mappings

Using Strong's Concordance

To look up Greek or Hebrew words:

  1. Navigate to any Bible verse
  2. Tap any word in the verse
  3. If Strong's data is available, you'll see the original language definition
  4. View the Strong's number (H#### for Hebrew, G#### for Greek)
  5. Read transliteration, pronunciation, and full definition

Search Tips

  • Search works across all installed translations
  • Results are grouped by match type (All Words, Any Word, Phrase)
  • Tap any result to jump directly to that verse

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this really free forever?

Yes. Thy Word is completely free with no subscriptions, no in-app purchases, and no advertisements. This is a ministry project to make God's Word accessible to everyone.

Does it work without internet?

Yes. Everything is stored on your device - the complete Bible, all translations, and the entire Strong's Concordance dictionary. Once installed, you never need an internet connection.

What is Strong's Concordance?

Strong's Concordance is a reference system that maps every word in the Bible to its original Hebrew or Greek meaning. Each word gets a unique number (H#### for Hebrew, G#### for Greek), allowing you to study the original language meanings even if you don't speak Hebrew or Greek.

Which Bible translations are included?

  • KJV - King James Version (1611)
  • WEB - World English Bible
  • ASV - American Standard Version
  • ARC - Almeida Revista e Corrigida (Portuguese)
  • LUTH1912 - Luther Bible (German)

How much storage does it use?

The complete app with all translations and Strong's data uses approximately 100MB of storage.

Do you collect any data?

No. Thy Word does not collect, transmit, or store any user data. There are no analytics, no tracking, and no telemetry. Your Bible reading and study is completely private.
